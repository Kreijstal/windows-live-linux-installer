Changing the icon

Using the Grub Menu ››

Change Icon






This screen will appear when you click the icon image on the Edit OS details screen.


The current icon will be highlighted in red. To make a change, simply click the icon you want to display at boot time. Your new selection will then be highlighted in red. In the example above, the manjaro icon is selected.


When you are satisfied with your choice. Click the Apply button.



Note: If you wish, you can add your own icons to Grub2Win.
